Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start using DRAKZ?

+

Getting started with DRAKZ is easy! Simply click on the "Let's Get Started" button on our homepage, create an account, and follow the guided setup process to begin managing your finances effectively.

Is my financial data secure with DRAKZ?

+

Yes, we take security very seriously. DRAKZ employs SSL/TLS encryption, multi-factor authentication, and secure hashing algorithms to ensure your financial data remains protected at all times.

What's the difference between free and premium accounts?

+

Free accounts provide access to basic financial dashboards, financial education, and our AI chatbot. Premium users enjoy advanced tools, ad-free experience, premium video courses, live sessions with advisors, and downloadable reports.

How do I become a financial advisor on DRAKZ?

+

To become a financial advisor on our platform, you must be a certified financial professional. Please contact us at advisors@drakz.com with your credentials, and our team will guide you through the verification and onboarding process.
